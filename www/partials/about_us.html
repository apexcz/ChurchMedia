About The Isaiah Wealth Ministries

Isaiah Wealth Ministries is an apostolic ministry with the mandate to prepare the way for the return of the Lord Jesus Christ.

This ministry is working hard in motivating and preparing the individuals believer to be involved in reaping the harvest of the last days. It runs several networks, programs and crusades back with the supernatural Signs and Wonders and manifestation of the tangible glory of God.

The outstanding revelatory grace given to Dr Wealth has provided great resources that have been of immerse blessing to the body of Christ. The ministry is active in church planting in almost every continent of the world through its Missions Assisted Project (MAP). This ministry focuses on reaching all nations of the earth, uniting ministers and providing spiritual covering and mentorship for pastors and churches all over the world.

About Gospel Pillars Ministry

Gospel Pillars Ministry incorporated is a young ministry which began in 2004 as a campus fellowship while its church ministry began in Lagos in 2007. The ministry which has a global vision presently has some thousands of members in an ever growing network of churches and campus fellowships in Nigeria and beyond the shores of Nigeria and Africa.

VISION: Giving witness of the true gospel to all nations in love and with great signs.

MISSION: Winning and equipping souls for the work of the ministry.

PURPOSE:Raising a people of all round success fulfilled in the service of Christ.

MOTTO: Happy People. Touching Lives...

SCRIPTURAL MOTIVATION: This gospel of the kingdom must be preached in all the world for a witness unto all nations, and then shall the end come (Matt. 24: 14).

OUR MESSAGE:We believe Christ through the holy Spirit in the heart of a man can bring about any change and the accomplishment of any vision or dream, power, sound mind, health, Prosperity, Holiness, etc. These workings are by Gods undiluted word which we preach & teach with all humility. To effectively aid the proclamation of the gospel of Christ we preach, the ministry developed various arms. They are:

Gospel Pillars Church (GPC) : This is the church arm of the ministry. It came as a result of the mandate in the words of the lord,'I have sent you to build an industry where men and women would be re-manufactured for success; a people that would find fulfilment in spreading the gospel to all the nations of the earth. They shall be many and of a militant spirit. Go raise them and teach men success.'

Gospel Pillars Campus Fellowship (GPCF) : This arm is for the campus ministry. Its vision is to reach out to students on campus by turning many unto righteousness while maintaining academic excellence.

Gospel Pillars Refinery Institute (GPRI) : This is the bible school of the ministry. Its goal is to raise labourers for the harvest field.

Michael Wealth Media and Outreach Ministry (MMOM) : This is the media arm of the ministry and its responsibility is to ensure that the gospel comes out in all audio, mp3 and video formats.

Michael Wealth Light Initiative (MLI) : MLI is the helps arm of the ministry. It provides food, shelter, medical services and scholarships to the needy in less fancied areas and ghetto.

The ministry also nurtures an Internet Ministry, Take Over Network (TON); a network for professionals who love the Lord and are passionate for success.

About Dr Isaiah Wealth

Dr Isaiah Wealth is a lover of Jesus and a man of great compassion for the sick and less privileged, author of several best sellers, song writer and a dynamic teacher, prophet, pastor and evangelist. He is indeed a man blessed with a coat of many colours.

His Ministry is characterized with great manifestation of miracles, the prophetic and supernatural appearance of gold dust - which he refers to as Golden Glory.

He is the president of Gospel Pillars Ministry which now operates an ever increasing network of over sixty chapters in over three continents including Africa, America and Europe. His passion to reach the world with the gospel is the centre of his message, missions and songs which through his record label, MCube is reshaping the sphere of christian music. He currently leads the International Christian Association of Ministers, whose vision is unity in the body of Christ via truth, holiness and service.

He earned a Doctor of Ministry degree from Trinity Graduate School of Apologetics, Coaching in 2013, and has been offered IICM membership in Florida USA.
